Science Communication

About My Science Communication

Because science is meant to be shared, I am passionate about science communication. I believe that sharing science with broad audiences is one of the most important missions of a researcher — both to educate people about science and to build empathy for the animals we study.

From podcasts and live events to social media and public talks, I try to bring animal cognition (and science more broadly) to as many people as possible.

Do not hesitate to reach out!
