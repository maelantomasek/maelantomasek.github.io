À propos

I am a doctor in comparative psychology (PhD) and in veterinary medicine (DVM). I study animal behaviour and cognition, with a focus on how animals perceive their world and on the evolution of cognitive abilities.

My research centres on wild Tanganyikan cichlids as a model system for understanding the ecology and evolution of cognition. I am equally invested in animal welfare and the ethics of animal research.

I am also an author — my debut novel Le Plafond (The Ceiling) was published in 2025.
