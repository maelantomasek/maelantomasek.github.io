Science Communication

About My Science Communication

In addition to my research, I am very involved in science communication. I believe that sharing science with broad audiences is one of the most important things a researcher can do — both to educate people about science and to build empathy for the animals we study.

From podcasts and live events to social media and public talks, I try to bring fish cognition (and science more broadly) to as many people as possible, in France and beyond.
