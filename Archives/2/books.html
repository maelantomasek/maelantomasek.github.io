About My Books

As an author, I am interested in linking science with imagination, to push the limits of what we know and to raise empathy. Combining my scientific background with storytelling, I write fiction that invites readers to question their assumptions about perception, difference, and the animal world.

Le Plafond — The Ceiling
Novel · 2025 French only

Le Plafond

Science-fiction exploring ethology and animal cognition

There, in the Around, danger lurks. In the Around, the inhabitants of the People lead a peaceful existence, suspended between the Ground and the Ceiling. But a threat is growing in the shadows: death is approaching. The Others are coming. Faced with this imminent danger, there is only one question. And there can be only one answer: are the Others allies or enemies?
